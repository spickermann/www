Martin Spickermann

My expertise extends to my prior experience at Envato in Melbourne and as Babbel's first Lead Developer.

As a dedicated member of the Ruby on Rails community, I have released multiple open-source libraries on Rubygems and contributed to Ruby on Rails. And I am an active participant on Stack Overflow, where I rank among the all-time top 20 answerers for both the Ruby programming language and the Ruby on Rails framework.
