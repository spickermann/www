Martin Spickermann

I am a Senior Software Engineer and enjoy building scalable web applications based on the Ruby on Rails framework since 2006.

Currently, I am the Principal Software Engineer at Localyze – working remotely from my home office in Bavaria, Germany.

Previously I was working at Shopify with one of the biggest and oldest Ruby on Rails applications in the world. I was a Lead Developer at the industry platform Zaikio GmbH, a Tech Lead at sonnen, a company in the renewable energy market, and the Lead Architect at the German FinTech Compeon. In the past, I worked at Envato in Melbourne (Australia) and at Babbel in Berlin.

It is important to give back to the community: I released several open–source libraries on Rubygems and contributed to Ruby on Rails. You will find me on Stack Overflow in the all-time top 20 answerers lists for the Ruby programming language and the Ruby on Rails web application framework.
